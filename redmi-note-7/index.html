About Note 7

aura design

Aura design, is an experience that surrounds you.  It’s an amalgamation of impeccable craftsmanship with  retained practicality to deliver an immersive experience to our Mi Fans. Immersive Experience Redmi Note series is today used by millions of Indians as their urban daily driver.  The all-new glass design houses 16cm (6.3) bright immersive FHD+ display with a dot  notch that pushes the boundaries of screen-to-body aspect ratio.

splash proof

To make the device splash-proof, we introduce the P2i nano-coating that will  repel accidental liquid spills and light rain. All the openings are now sealed with  custom rubberized material to keep any liquid from seeping in.

Dot notch full screen display

The Dot Notch Display comes as the latest innovation aimed at maximizing the Full Screen Display experience.  The design features the front camera without compromising on screen space. The FHD+ 2340 x 1080 resolution enriches your viewing experience by delivering crisp visuals and sharper details. The high screen-to-body ratio results in a truly refined viewing experience.
